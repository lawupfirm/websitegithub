## How It Works

1. **Contact Form**: User fills form → Verifies email → Clicks "Pay Consultation Fee"
2. **Payment Modal Opens**: Shows all services OR custom amount option
3. **Select Service**: Click on desired service, fee appears
4. **Review Amount**: See total amount to pay
5. **Complete Payment**: Click "Pay ₹XXXX"
6. **Razorpay Modal**: Complete payment via card/UPI/wallet
7. **Instant Receipt**: Email receipt sent automatically
8. **Follow-up**: Team contacts within 24 business hours
